$‫\therefore \text{ج} = \{ 50, 40, 30, 20, 10 \} \Leftarrow \text{ج} = 5‬$‫

‬$‫\therefore \text{حا (ج)} = \frac{\text{ج (ج)}}{\text{ج (ج)}} \quad \therefore \text{حا (ج)} = \frac{5}{10}‬$‫

وحيث إن: حا (ج) = ١ - حا (ج).

‬$‫\therefore \text{حا (ج)} = 1 - \text{حا (ج)} = 1 - \frac{1}{10} = \frac{9}{10}‬$
