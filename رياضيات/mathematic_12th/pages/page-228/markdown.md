الحل :

استناداً إلى الصيغة (٧-٤) :

أ) $$\sum_{i=1}^n (m^2 - 4m) \cdot (n) = \sum_{i=1}^n (m^2 - 4m)$$

$$= 27 - 18 + 1 = 10$$

$$= \sum_{i=1}^n (m^2 - 4m) = \sum_{i=1}^n (m^2 - 4m)$$

$$= \sum_{i=1}^n (m^2 - 4m) = \frac{14}{3}$$

$$\therefore \sum_{i=1}^n (m - 2) \cdot (n) = \sum_{i=1}^n (m - 2) \cdot (n) = \sum_{i=1}^n (m - 2) \cdot (n) = \sum_{i=1}^n (m - 2)$$
